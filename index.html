About JVIT

Premier Institute of Technical Education

Janavikas Institute of Technology (JVIT) is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among students. Established in 2001, JVIT has emerged as a leading institution in technical education.

Our campus spread over 25 acres provides a serene and conducive environment for learning with state-of-the-art infrastructure including modern laboratories, a central library with digital resources, sports facilities, and hostel accommodation.

JVIT is approved by AICTE and affiliated to VTU, committed to providing value-based education and transforming students into competent professionals.
